We want to build a blockforce game in spirit of popular shooting game sky force.
The game starts with a 2d maps display all stages that is available to be unlocked and a hanger/garage where user can select and upgrade planes.
To unlock a new stage, you have beat previous stage and meet some medal counts.
Each stage also have easy, normal and hard level, featuring same enemiies but with higher health/damage/faster fire rate. 
There are 3 types of medals you can earn, 1st is destroy all enimies, 2nd is not get hit, 3rd is collect all stars.
When you destroy an enenmy, a random of amount of stars will pop out the the enemy and slowly drift down and out of screen, you have collect them.
You could upgrade your main canon damage, missles, manget(help you attract stars towards you so it is easiler to collect).
There are a few types of plane user can unlock and choose from. with classic being a default choice available at open and others you can unlock by collect parts that is randomed drop from enemies just like the stars. 
The drop rate of the parts are roughly 50% per level based on stage and level
The classic plane is a balanced red color plane that shoots fire ball out of the front tip main canon and misles from the wings.
The V-Force plane is a voilet color plane shoots out bullets in a V shape from its from tip.
The Octupus plane is a light bluee plane that shoots mini missles instead of main canon.
The difference between main canon and missles are that missles are self guided weapon and have a lowe fire rate than the canon.
